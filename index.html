How to use the site

Uploading Images

Click the "TEST IMAGES" button and either browse to your files or drag-and-drop them into the drop zone. You can upload as many files as you wish at a time. If you've already uploaded some images and you'd like to upload more, click the "check more images" button at the top of the screen.
